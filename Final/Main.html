Name Warframe The Apothecary Diaries
Google Summaries Warframe is a free-to-play action role-playing third-person shooter multiplayer online game developed and published by Digital Extremes. Trained from an early age by her apothecary father, Mao Mao is sold as a servant to the emperor's palace. Mao Mao now puts her knowledge at the service of the people of the palace, not only to treat them but also to foil mysteries and conspiracies.
Related info The official website can be found here. No official site exists to serve as a center of information beyond the Wiki entries or fandom.
Personal thoughts This game has lasted 12 years for a reason. There is a thriving community with developers in constant contact with the players. While some parts can trigger motion sickness, I wish I started playing sooner. This series is a fun read with many mysteries to uncover, while being placed within a setting similar to feudal times. There are 15 light novels to read, or two seasons of the anime to watch with the third being announced recently.
